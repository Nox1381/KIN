Save Food. Save Money. Save the Planet.

Join our mission to reduce food waste and enjoy delicious meals at a fraction of the price.

Our Goal

Our mission is simple: connect people with perfectly good surplus food from restaurants and grocery stores, reducing waste and helping the community access affordable meals. Together, we can make a difference — for our planet, for local businesses, and for everyone’s table.

How It Works

1. Browse Vendors

Find local shops and restaurants offering surplus food at discounted prices.

2. Choose Your Bag

Pick a surprise bag filled with perfectly good food that would otherwise go to waste.

3. Pick Up & Enjoy

Collect your food at the scheduled time and enjoy your meal while saving the planet.
